\subsection{P234-20}
已知数据见表，试求拟合公式$y=a + b x^2$
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{c|c c c c c}
$x_i$ & 19   & 25   & 31   & 38   & 44 \\
\hline
$y_i$ & 19.0 & 32.3 & 49.0 & 73.3 & 97.8
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
\begin{SOLVE}
在Matlab命令窗口执行
\begin{lstlisting}
>> x = [19 25 31 38 44]; y = [19.0 32.3 49.0 73.3 97.8]; xx = x.*x

xx =

         361         625         961        1444        1936

>> polyfit(xx,y,1)

ans =

    0.0500    0.9726
\end{lstlisting}
于是所求拟合公式为
\begin{displaymath}
y = 0.9726 + 0.0500 x^2
\end{displaymath}
\end{SOLVE}
