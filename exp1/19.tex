\subsection{P233-19}
已知数据见表，试求拟合公式$y=a e^{b x}$
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{c|c c c c c c c c}
$x_i$ & 1    & 2    & 3    & 4    & 5    & 6    & 7    & 8 \\
\hline
$y_i$ & 15.3 & 20.5 & 27.4 & 34.6 & 49.1 & 65.6 & 87.8 & 117.6
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
\begin{SOLVE}
在Matlab命令窗口执行
\begin{lstlisting}
>> x = [1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8]; y = [15.3 20.5 27.4 34.6 49.1 65.6 87.8 117.6]; ly = log(y);
>> polyfit(x,ly,1)

ans =

    0.2919    2.4268

>> exp(2.4268)

ans =

   11.3229
\end{lstlisting}
于是所求拟合公式为
\begin{displaymath}
y = 11.3226 e^{0.2919 x}
\end{displaymath}
\end{SOLVE}
